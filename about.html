Background

PLAYED IT.
NOW COACHING IT.

Nxt Rep Basketball is run by Yianni — a Sydney-based point guard with professional playing experience in Greece and Serbia. After years competing at that level, he returned to Australia with one goal: to bring a genuinely professional coaching standard to NSW basketball.

Too many players in Australia don't get exposure to the structure, detail and intentionality that defines European basketball development. That gap is exactly what Nxt Rep is here to fill.

Alongside Nxt Rep, Yianni serves as Coaching Director at Maccabi Basketball NSW — working across junior pathways and senior programmes to build a system that develops players from the ground up.

Philosophy

HOW WE THINK
ABOUT BASKETBALL

01

INTENTIONAL REPS

There's no such thing as casual improvement. Every session has a purpose, every drill has a reason, and every player leaves knowing what they worked on and why.

02

BASKETBALL IQ FIRST

Skills matter — but the players who make it are the ones who understand the game. We teach decision-making, spacing, reads and the mental side alongside every physical skill.

03

EUROPEAN STRUCTURE

Australian basketball is athletic. European basketball is systematic. The best players are both. We bring the structure and detail from a European background and apply it here.

04

PLAYER BEFORE GAME

We develop people, not just players. Accountability, work ethic, communication and coachability are built into every programme — because those skills transfer beyond basketball.

05

LONG-TERM PATHWAYS

Short-term wins are nice. Long-term development is the goal. We build programmes that take players from juniors to seniors — not just one good season.

06

COMPETE EVERY DAY

Training should feel like competing. We create environments where players are challenged, accountable and driven — so when game day comes, it's familiar.
