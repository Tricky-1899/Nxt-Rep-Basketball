Background

PLAYED IT.
NOW COACHING IT.

Nxt Rep Basketball is run by Yianni — a Sydney-based point guard with professional playing experience in Greece and Serbia. After years competing at that level, he returned to Australia with one goal: to bring a genuinely professional coaching standard to NSW basketball.

Too many players in Australia don't get exposure to the structure and detail that defines European basketball development. That gap is exactly what Nxt Rep is here to fill.

Alongside Nxt Rep, Yianni serves as Coaching Director at Maccabi Basketball NSW.
