About

COACHING BUILT ON REAL EXPERIENCE

Nxt Rep Basketball is run by Yianni — a point guard who played professionally in Greece and Serbia, and now brings that same professional-level intensity to coaching in NSW.

The philosophy is simple: basketball isn't just about reps. It's about the right reps — intentional, progressive training that builds the habits serious players need to compete at the next level.
