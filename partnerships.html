How It Works

FROM ENQUIRY
TO FIRST SESSION

01

REACH OUT

Send us a message via the contact page. Tell us about your school or centre, your term dates, and roughly how many students you're looking to enrol. That's all we need to start.

02

INITIAL CONSULTATION

We'll have a quick call to understand your specific setup — space available, age groups, term structure — and put together a program proposal tailored to your situation.

03

PROGRAM DESIGN

A full term plan is built: session outlines, skill progressions and enrolment details. You review it, we refine it if needed — then it's locked in.

04

LAUNCH & DELIVER

We show up, we run the program, and we handle everything on the ground. You get regular updates and full support throughout the term.
