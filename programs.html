The Process

WHAT TO EXPECT
FROM DAY ONE

01

INITIAL CONVERSATION

Every engagement starts with a conversation. We want to understand your goals, your current level and what success actually looks like for you — before a single session happens.

02

ASSESSMENT SESSION

For private training and team coaching, we run an assessment first. Not a test — an opportunity to see where you are and build the right programme from that baseline.

03

CUSTOM PLAN

A structured plan is put together based on that conversation and assessment. Every plan has a goal, a timeline and a measurable outcome built in.

04

CONSISTENT EXECUTION

Then we get to work. Every session is intentional. Progress is tracked. Plans evolve as you improve. There's no coasting — but there's also clear reward for the work you put in.
